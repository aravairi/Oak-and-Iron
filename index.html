About Oak & Iron

I'm a high schooler in 11th grade who started Oak & Iron to learn, build, and give back. I design and craft every piece by hand, focusing on classic forms, durable joinery, and a natural, earth-toned aesthetic.

My goal is to use the profits from my work to fund donation benches and tables for local parks. Each project brings me closer to helping create welcoming spaces in our community.
